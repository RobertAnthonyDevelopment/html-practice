These solutions are tested across multiple stages including development, testing, and production environments. His expertise extends to developing intricate testing procedures specifically for the Moodle LMS, focusing on course and topic creation processes that cater to both in-person field and agile virtual training environments. Furthermore, Clemons has innovatively developed a client-facing Course of Action presentation system and a comprehensive Octane PM testing process.
